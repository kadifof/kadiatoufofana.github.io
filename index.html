Global Mental Health Analysis
In Power BI

The "Mental Health Analysis" report highlights key insights into treatment seeking rates, stress levels, and openness to mental health discussions across different months and demographics. Notably, the data reveals peaks in treatment seeking at 100% for several months and significant gender disparities in stress reporting, with males experiencing higher stress. Variations in openness to mental health interviews suggest potential stigma, while diverse indoor behavior patterns reflect different lifestyle impacts on mental health. The analysis emphasizes the need for data integrity checks, gender-specific interventions, and tailored behavioral health support to enhance mental health awareness and treatment efficacy.
